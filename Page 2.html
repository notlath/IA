Executive Summary

D.I.C.K.S. Pro is a digital service that involves community support groups collaborating to provide an exceptional digital learning environment for all. Through the inclusion of its diverse training courses, D.I.C.K.S. Pro envisions digital citizens geared with bonafide digital information and supporting computer knowledge, complementing steps towards the future of the digital world. Most Filipinos have shifted from being company workers to freelancers as data has revealed that 32% of freelancers claim that demand has grown as a result of the COVID-19 epidemic, and the number of full-time freelancers surged from 17% to 28% between 2014 and 2019. Not only has the demand for freelancers increased, but consumption habits have changed immensely, with 67% preferring e-commerce over traditional forms of purchasing. With the development of online sales and commercial activity, individuals have been enticed to attempt an online business that is now renowned, and which is why many companies have opted to hire employees who can market their products on social media. Hence how we founded our company and chose our five (5) training courses.

We are receptive to exploring prospective profit-making hobbies at this age since we are at home with technological resources. Opening up additional job/freelance options for individuals who are eager to learn and advance with technology trends would be the first step. Learning and improving abilities might be a viable method to earn money. With this, our team created a service that teaches internet and computer technology intricacies in simple ways. D.I.C.K.S. Pro will be home-based and our team will use social media, specifically our Facebook page, to advertise the courses we will offer. Customers will simply submit a message on our page if they have any questions or want to subscribe to our training courses. The instruction would be delivered through exclusive video lessons to which only subscribers would have access. And as what our company aims, our service will be available to people of all ages as here at D.I.C.K.S Pro, learning has no barriers.

Evidently, D.I.C.K.S. Pro includes a variety of competitive elements that may undoubtedly provide Filipinos with a one-of-a-kind learning experience. With its exact and comprehensive course offerings, the price of the size of its services is based on the notion of affordability in order to connect with more people and accomplish D.I.C.K.S. Pro's goals and objectives. The company's initial capitalization is Php 9700, with non-recurring licenses/permits.
